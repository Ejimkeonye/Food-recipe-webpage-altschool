1. Fluffy Classic Pancakes ü•û

The perfect morning breakfast treat. These pancakes are light, airy, and stackable!

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 5 min | Cook Time: 15 min | Servings: 8 pancakes | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar into a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
Source: Cafe Delites

2. Crispy Chicken Sandwich üçî

A fast-food favorite made better at home with a crunchy coating and tangy sauce.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 min | Cook Time: 10 min | Servings: 2 sandwiches | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 2 brioche buns

Instructions

  1. Slice chicken breasts in half lengthwise to create four thin cutlets.
  2. Marinate the chicken in the buttermilk and hot sauce for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Mix flour, paprika, and cayenne in a shallow dish. Dredge the chicken pieces completely in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat cooking oil (1/2 inch deep) in a pan to 350¬∞F. Fry the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Toast the buns, add your sauce (mayo or aioli), and assemble the sandwich. Enjoy immediately!
Source: Swasthi's Recipes

3. No-Bake Cheesecake üç∞

A simple, creamy dessert that requires no oven time‚Äîperfect for hot days!

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 min | Chill Time: 4 hours | Servings: 10 slices | Difficulty: Very Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Instructions

  1. Combine graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish.
  2. Beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  3. Gradually beat in the powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
  4. Fold in the whipped heavy cream until the mixture is light and airy.
  5. Pour the filling into the crust. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
Source: Sugar Spun Run
